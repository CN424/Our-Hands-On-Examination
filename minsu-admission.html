Admission Requirements

Qualification for Mindoro State University: Bongabong Campus

You are qualified for admission, if;

1. You have completed senior high school.

2. You are high school graduate on or before March 2015.

3. You are ALS certified eligible for admission to College

Additional Qualification:

1. Must not have taken any college subject/s previously;

2. Must not be taking any college subject/s at present; and

3. Students enrolled in vocational courses or those not leading to a degree programs can apply for the Mindoro State University: Bongabong Campus

Documentary Requirements:

For Freshmen College:

1. Form 138(Report Card)

2. Certificate of Good Moral Character

3. Authenticated (NSO/PSA) Birth Certificate.

4. 4pcs. Identical 2x2 colored ID pictures white background

5. 4pcs. Identical 1x1 colored ID pictures white background

For Transferee:

Honorable Dismissal/Transfer Credential

1. Form 138(Report Card)

2. Official Transcript of Records

3. Authenticated (NSO/PSA) Birth Certificate.

4. 4 pcs. Identical 2x2 colored ID pictures white background

5. 4 pcs. Identical 1x1 colored ID pictures white background

6. 1 long white folder

How To Qualify For An Undergraduate Degree Program:

To qualify in a particular undergraduate degree program, the applicant needs to choose from among the list of courses offered in the College. The applicant needs to indicate the choice of course with its corresponding course code in the MinSCAT College admission Test Application Form.

Laboratory High School Department

MinSCAT maintains Laboratory High School that serves as training ground for the student teachers. The Secondary Department adopts the Basic Education Curriculum (BEC)
